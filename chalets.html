Chalet (Cabins) options to choose from

here are some features about each of our 3 chalets

Kākāpo

- Our Kākāpo chalet is the Largest of the chalets you'll be able to hire as a member.

- It features a Large kitchen, dining and living area for you.

- What truly makes it amazing is that it provides everyone comfort, convenience, and pleasure.

Pūkeko

- Our Pūkeko chalet is Great for people coming in large groups/families wanting a chalet to themselves.

- This limits on adult/child quantity to ensure you guys a nice comfortable stay.

Kererū

- Our Kererū chalet is a greatest option for a small family

- This Chalet has 2 nice and comfy bedrooms to ensure a nice cosy rest.

- This Perfect for families with 2-4children based on the size of the chalet.

- This features a Separate living area filled with a nice coach, skis and a dinning table.

More Info

Only Club members can access the chalets.

All chalet bookings must be from Friday to Sunday.

Chalets offer shared accommodation for between 6 and 40 people per chalet.

All three chalets are on the mountain, so you can ski to and from the chalets.

The mountain gives 550 hectares of skiing/snowboarding area, and the longest ski/snowboard season in New Zealand.
